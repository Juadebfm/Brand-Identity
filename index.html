Typography

We used a variety of brand fonts to express our open, confident personality.

  • Raleway, sans-serif

Raleway is our sans-serif web fonts of choice. It has a bit more personality than a classic sans-serif typeface like Arial or Verdana, with its geometric styled lettering. The Raleway Font Family has 9 weights, making this a great option for bold headers or subheaders, as well as legible but personable body copy font. This font is available on both Google Fonts and it's load time is relatively faster than most geometric styled lettering.
